About IRISTEM

Student-led STEM for safer, more resilient communities

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
IRISTEM is a student-led nonprofit that translates peer-reviewed research in climate, resilience, disaster preparedness, environmental safety, and STEM into age-appropriate learning resources.
We partner with schools and community groups to deliver hands-on learning, mentorship, and local research projects that connect science to practical action.
We envision a world where every young person can understand environmental risks, participate in STEM, and help build a safer future.

About IRISTEM

Connecting advanced research with younger learners

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
IRISTEM is a student-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit that translates complex scientific work into clear curriculum and outreach materials for younger students.
IRISTEM helps researchers meet their Broader Impacts goals by translating complex STEM research into accessible, student-centered curriculum and outreach materials.
We envision more young people encountering meaningful research early, with the context and learning tools they need to understand why it matters.

What We Do

Supporting education-focused Broader Impacts

Research Translation

We turn peer-reviewed research into accessible lessons, workshops, and outreach resources.

Curriculum Development

Lessons, worksheets, vocabulary, and discussion guides turn findings into classroom-ready learning.

Hands-On Activities

Activities help students investigate scientific ideas through observation, design, and discussion.

Youth Outreach

Resources support schools, youth organizations, workshops, and community education partners.

Visual Communication

Visual summaries and clear language give students approachable entry points into advanced topics.

Impact Documentation

Participation data, surveys, and summaries help describe educational reach and engagement.

Our Work

See how IRISTEM translates research into explainers, activities, and outreach resources that younger students can understand and use.

Our Work

A clear path from research to outreach

1. Understand

We review papers, reports, presentations, and project goals to identify the essential ideas and intended impact.

2. Translate

Student creators turn technical material into clear narratives, visuals, vocabulary, lessons, and activities.

3. Engage

Completed resources support classroom learning, youth outreach, workshops, and community education.

What We Create

Resources designed for real learning settings

Youth-Friendly Explainers

Clear introductions to research questions, findings, vocabulary, and real-world applications.

Classroom Resources

Curriculum, worksheets, discussion guides, and activities for different grade levels.

Coastal Resilience

Student-facing resources can connect erosion, sea-level rise, and community safety to clear scientific explanations.

Wildfire Education

Visual explainers and activities help students understand risk, preparedness, and environmental change.

Water and Drought

Lessons can turn climate and water research into practical context for younger learners.

Outreach Experiences

Materials for workshops, community programs, school visits, and youth-facing events.

Impact Summaries

Participation data, survey insights, and concise reporting that describe educational engagement.

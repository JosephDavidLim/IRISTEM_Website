IRISTEM SCHOOL LOGO PACK
==========================

Contents
- standardized_png/: 18 transparent 1024 x 1024 PNG canvases, one per school
- raw/: original downloaded image files
- IRISTEM_School_Logos_Contact_Sheet.png: quick visual index
- logo_sources.csv: source, logo type, original dimensions, and quality notes

Important notes
- Several schools expose an athletics or mascot mark more readily than a formal institutional seal. The source type is identified in logo_sources.csv.
- The Saratoga, Presentation, Folsom, Santa Teresa, Sierra, and Granada images came from small athletics-profile thumbnails and were upscaled for normal website display. They are not suitable for large-format printing.
- Logos and school marks remain the property of their respective schools and organizations. Before public or commercial use, follow each school's brand rules and request permission where required.
- Do not stretch, recolor, crop, or otherwise distort the logos.

Student-Led by Design

Students translating STEM for students

IRISTEM brings a student perspective to research communication. Our team works to preserve scientific meaning while making unfamiliar ideas easier for younger audiences to understand.

Students contribute through research review, writing, curriculum design, visual communication, outreach, and chapter leadership.

About IRISTEM

Research made accessible to the next generation

  • Who We Are
  • Mission
  • Approach
IRISTEM is a student-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit that works with researchers, universities, and science organizations to make advanced STEM research understandable and useful for younger students.
IRISTEM helps researchers meet their Broader Impacts goals by translating complex STEM research into accessible, student-centered curriculum and outreach materials.
Our student team studies research papers, reports, and project materials, then develops clear explainers, classroom resources, activities, visuals, and outreach tools for youth audiences.

Why It Matters

Bridging research and public understanding

Accessible Education

We translate technical findings into age-appropriate resources for elementary, middle, and high school learners.

Broader Reach

We help research teams connect their work with schools, youth organizations, and community audiences.

Student Perspective

Materials created by students for students make advanced topics more approachable, relevant, and engaging.

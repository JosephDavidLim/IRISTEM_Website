About Us

IRISTEM is a student-led nonprofit that translates peer-reviewed research in climate, resilience, disaster preparedness, environmental safety, and STEM into age-appropriate learning resources.

We turn real research into lessons, workshops, and outreach toolkits that students and educators can actually use—connecting scientific knowledge to practical community action across 37 countries.

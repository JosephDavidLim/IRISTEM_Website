About IRISTEM

IRISTEM is a student-led organization focused on environmental safety through STEM education.

We partner with schools and community groups to deliver hands-on learning, mentorship, and local research projects.

Our Mission & Focus

Our Mission & Focus

What We Do

Advocacy and community-based action for environmental safety—amplifying student voices, sharing research, and promoting safer technology practices.

Who We Serve

Youth and communities disproportionately affected by environmental and technological risks, especially those with limited access to STEM opportunities.

Our Approach

Student-led action in partnership with schools and youth organizations, grounded in research and community feedback.
